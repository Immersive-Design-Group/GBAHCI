Pre-CHI 2026

Call for Participants

We offer four different ways to participate. Registration is free for everyone.
Please fill out the corresponding questionnaire if you wish to participate in a specific category.

Paper Presentations

Authors of accepted papers at top-tier HCI venues (e.g., CHI 2026, CSCW 2025) are invited to share their work. This is a prime opportunity to engage with the local community and practice your talk before the main conference.

Deadline: March 7, 2026

Registration

Poster Presentations

Showcase your works-in-progress, late-breaking results, or early-stage ideas. Poster sessions provide a dynamic environment for in-depth discussions, gathering valuable feedback, and fostering future collaborations.

Deadline: March 7, 2026

Conference Booklet Flyers

Ideal for labs, schools, or academic programs looking to increase their visibility. Promote your research group, recruit students, or showcase your program to a targeted audience of HCI researchers and practitioners.

Note: This service is free for educational organizations within GreaterBayHCI. For commercial or other institutional advertising inquiries, please contact Seungwoo Je.

Deadline: March 7, 2026

Participant Registration

Open to all attendees! Even if you are not presenting, you are warmly invited to join the community. Engage in inspiring discussions, network with fellow researchers, and explore the latest HCI trends in the Greater Bay Area.
